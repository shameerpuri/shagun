Wedding Reception

The wedding reception is the most important event in a wedding. It constitutes the focal point of the whole wedding because it serves as the common platform for the guests and hosts to come together and sink in the series of fun and merrimentWedding Receptionwith a lavish spread of gourmet cuisine and drinks at their disposal. The whole series of meals to be served at the wedding, be it the evening snacks, scrumptiouscake, dining, late night supper for stay-on guests etc has to be outlined in the invitation card itself so there’s no room for any confusion as to what to expect at the reception. Each and every meal has to be meticulously planned so that there are no unsatisfied guests at the end.

Now, putting the things together to make a wedding reception memorable is painstakingly elaborate and time consuming considered from the angle of a layman. But, it is not so with professional help at your hand. From calculating the cost per plate with accompanied beverages for each meal to the cost involved in laying the tables with decoration for the occasion, we can give you a forecast as to how much you would be spending on each guest apart from a lump sum kept aside for any unseen expenditure. With our rich experience in this line, it’s nothing more than a cakewalk for us to arrange for a flowing wedding reception with all the necessary details thrown in at the right degrees.
