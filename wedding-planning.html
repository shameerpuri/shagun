Wedding Planning

An event of the magnitude of a wedding ceremony demands professional assistance for a hassle free and smooth arrangement of all the aspects of the wedding right from the inception of the idea to its final conclusion.

We provide customized assistance for the following services:

  • Setting the event budget in consultation with our clients: We guide and assist our clients with the chalking out of the budget for an event. Our expertise lies in streamlining the expenditure account so as to cover all essential functions while leaving no room for compromise under any head.
  • Arranging the decoration of the venue: Decorating a venue as per the occasion necessitates an eye for details which we have in plenty owing to our long standing experience in this line.
  • Arranging the necessary catering services for the event: From choosing the right catering service providers to guiding them about the most suitable menu for the occasion is bound to ensure a happy, well-fed bunch of returning guests.
  • Arranging for professional entertainment package: The entertainment factor in an event makes it memorable. We have a whole bunch of professional entertainers in our kitty to help you liven up the whole show.
  • Supplying designer clothes for the wedding couple as well as other family members as per requirement: Our connectivity with the who’s who of the fashion fraternity across all budget segments gives us an inherent confidence to assure our clients a truly fashion packed affair.
  • Make-up: Last but not the least, the beautification of the people involved in an event is duly taken care of with our expert lineup of the most happening and reputed spas and beautician backed parlours across India.

Fitting all the above aspects of an event into the given budget and making it all affordable to our clients is our special background role as an established event planner.
